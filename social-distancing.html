Social Distancing Drive-Thru

Our renowned potatoes are available every day at our Farm Shop in Outrath. Post code E21 FN26.

On Saturdays we will operate a Drive-Thru system at our Farm Shop from 9am to 1pm.

You will be able to collect Golden Wonders, Roosters, Kerr Pinks, and Records without having to leave the car.

Contactless payment will be available.

We are doing this to encourage social distancing and to make our customers feel more comfortable.

Trading will continue as normal at all other times.

We are starting the Drive-Thru to facilitate our many loyal customers who purchased our potatoes at Cahir Farmers Market.
