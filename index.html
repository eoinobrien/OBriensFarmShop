Welcome, we're O'Brien's Farm Shop

We're a family run farm, who specialize in growing the very best in Tipperary potatoes. We operate a farm shop, stocking the very best of local produce, located on the old Dublin Road (R639), between Cahir and New Inn. We are also a founding member of Cahir Farmer's Market and have held a stall there every Saturday, since 2003.

Top Quality Produce

We grow quality Tipperary potatoes, including the customer favorite, the flowery Golden Wonder. We the best of local in season produce also, including, award winning local Jams, Jellys, World Cup Winning Honey and every Saturday, at the market, the very best in local seasonal vegetables.
