We are permanently closed

As of 2021, we have decided to stop growing potatoes, and as such we have closed our farm shop and market stall.

We would like to thank every one of our customers for your support over the many decades.

We're a family run farm, who specialized in growing the very best in Tipperary potatoes. We operated a farm shop, stocking the very best of local produce, located on the old Dublin Road (R639), between Cahir and New Inn. We are also a founding member of Cahir Farmer's Market and have held a stall there every Saturday, since 2003.

Cahir Farmer's Market is still operating every Saturday, and we recommend a visit to purchase the very best of local produce.
